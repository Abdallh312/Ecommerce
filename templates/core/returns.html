Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Nova Essentials purchase.

14-Day Return Window

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in their original condition and packaging. Please note the specific conditions for returnable and non-returnable items below.

RETURNABLE ITEMS

  • Unworn items with original tags
  • Items in original packaging
  • Standard (non-custom) products

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items worn, washed or damaged
  • Items without original tags

The Return Process

01

Contact our support team via email or phone.

02

Receive return authorization and instructions.

03

Package items securely in original packaging.

04

Ship items back using the provided instructions.
